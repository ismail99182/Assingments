Chicken Korma

Chicken Korma is a traditional South Asian dish made with chicken,
yogurt, and a mix of fragrant spices. It’s rich, creamy,
and full of flavor—perfect for a special meal.

Ingredients:

You will need chicken, onions, garlic, ginger, yogurt, oil,
and a blend of spices including coriander, cumin, and garam masala.

Instructions:

Start by frying onions until golden, then add garlic, ginger,
and spices. Add chicken and cook until browned. Mix in yogurt and let it simmer until the chicken is tender and the oil separates.

Preparation Time:

Preparation time is around 15 minutes.
Cooking time is approximately 30–40 minutes.
