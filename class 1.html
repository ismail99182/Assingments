Ultimate Fitness Guide

Fitness is not just about working out; it’s a lifestyle
includes physical activity, balanced nutrition, and mental well-being.
This guide will help you start your fitness journey.

Why Fitness is Important?

Regular exercise improves your physical health, boosts your mood, increases energy levels,
and helps you maintain a healthy weight. It also reduces the risk of chronic diseases.

1. Exercise

Include a mix of cardio (like running, walking, or cycling), strength training (like push-ups or lifting weights),
and flexibility exercises (like yoga or stretching).

2. Nutrition

Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins,
whole grains, and healthy fats. Avoid excessive sugar and processed foods.

3. Rest & Recovery

Your body needs time to repair and grow stronger. Make sure you get 7–8 hours of sleep
and take rest days between intense workouts.
